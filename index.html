A short bio

I am post-doc researcher at the Artificial Intelligence Research Institute. In the field of machine learning, my research works have been related to learning problems with weak supervision. Our proposals aim to learn predictive models, specifically Bayesian network classifiers, from this kind of data.

I did my PhD studies at the Intelligent Systems Group of the University of the Basque Country under the supervision of Iñaki Inza and Jose A. Lozano.
